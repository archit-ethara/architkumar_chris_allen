# Heartbeat: Chris Allen

## Recurring Events

### Daily

- **4:30 AM**: Wake. Coffee, paddle prep or dryland prep depending on the day.
- **10:00 PM**: Lights out. Reading or quiet time on the lanai before bed.

### Weekly

- **Monday**: 5:00 to 6:30 AM paddling practice at Ala Wai Canal with Koa Kai. 8:15 AM at Palmside. Full operational day through 5:30 to 6:00 PM. Home by 6:30. Evening workshop or reading.
- **Tuesday**: 5:00 to 6:30 AM dryland training at home. 8:15 AM at Palmside. Full operational day. Evening workshop or reading.
- **Wednesday**: 5:00 to 6:30 AM paddling practice at Ala Wai Canal. 8:15 AM at Palmside. 3:00 PM biweekly therapy with Dr. Mark Sullivan when scheduled. 6:30 to 8:00 PM visit Dorothy at Plumeria Gardens.
- **Thursday**: 5:00 to 6:30 AM dryland training at home. 8:15 AM at Palmside. Full operational day. Evening workshop or reading.
- **Friday**: 5:00 to 6:30 AM paddling practice at Ala Wai Canal. 8:15 AM at Palmside. Full operational day. Evening with Mike Kamaka or quiet dinner at home.
- **Saturday**: 5:30 to 7:30 AM paddle practice with Koa Kai. Open-water swim after. Afternoon errands, visit Sarah and Hana, or 2nd-Saturday Oenophile Circle wine club at 6:00 PM.

### Quarterly
