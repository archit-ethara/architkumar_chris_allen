POLICIES EFFECTIVE FROM DAY ONE

Uniform and appearance: Your uniform must be worn and pressed for every shift. Name tag must be worn at all times on property. Hair must be neat and off the face for guest-facing roles.

Guest privacy: Do not share any information about guests with other guests or anyone outside the property. This includes room numbers, names, or plans. Violations result in immediate disciplinary action.

Social media: Do not post photos of guests, colleagues, or hotel interiors without written approval from the GM office. Guest photos are never permitted.

Timekeeping: Clock in and out using the kiosk on your floor. Clocking in for another employee is a terminable offense.

Guest complaints: Never argue with a guest. Acknowledge, apologize, and escalate immediately to your supervisor. You are empowered to offer a complimentary water or coffee while the guest waits for a resolution.

Cell phones: Personal phones must be on silent and kept out of sight during your shift. Use is permitted during your break in designated break areas only.

EMPLOYEE BENEFITS SUMMARY

Medical insurance: Eligible after 30 days. Enrollment forms due to HR by day 25.
Paid time off: Accrual begins after 90 days. Rate is 0.038 hours per hour worked.
Meal credit: One complimentary meal per shift in the team member cafeteria.
Parking: Validated staff parking available in the Kuhio Garage, Level 2. Display your staff badge on the dashboard.
Hotel discount: 30 percent discount on hotel rates for personal travel after 90 days, subject to availability.
